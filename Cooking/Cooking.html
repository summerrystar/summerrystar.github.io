1. 买肋排或带点肥的猪肉，煎至金黄（不用放油），放盐、酱油、酒，煎好盛出备用

1. Choose baby ribs or pork chops with some fat. Pan fry pork till golden (no need for oil). Put salt, soy sauce and rice wine. Remove from pan after ready.

2. 同一个锅下番茄炒软出汁（加盐），加开水，加猪肉煮开，焖10分钟

2. In the same pan, pan fry tomatos till it's soft and juicy (put salt). Add boiling water and pork. Let it boil with small fire for 10 minutes.

3. 尝盐度，补足盐后加白菜（不然白菜没味道），再焖5分钟直到白菜变软

3. Taste and add salt as needed, then add napa cabbage (add salt first or napa may not have enough taste). Boil with small fire for 5 more minutes till napa gets soft.

4. 可以加面条一起吃

4. Optionally add noodles

1. 蘑菇与米放进电饭锅一起煮，煮完加盐

1. Cook rice and mushroom together in the rice cooker. Add salt when finished

2. 煮饭的同时煎三文鱼：先把鱼皮煎到脆，然后可以捣碎了煎。调料用蒜盐、莳萝、一点柠檬汁。

2. While rice is getting cooked, pan fry salmon: First the skin side till crispy, then break into small pieces. Use garlic salt, dill and a sprinkle of lemon juice.

3. 组装三文鱼、蘑菇饭、足量葱花。三文鱼煎出的油恰好能使米饭颗粒分明。

3. Stir together salmon, mushroom rice, and enough chopped green onions. The oil produced while pan-frying the salmon can make rice grains separate from each other.

1. 鱼两面撒上蒜盐

1. Sprinkle garlic salt to both sides of the fish

2. 热锅内放食用油、黄油、蒜泥，下鱼，两面充分混合，煎至金黄。如果中间还没熟，再用小火焖一会儿

2. In a hot pan, put oil, butter, chopped garlic, put in fish, mix both sides properly, pan-fry till golden. If the inside is still raw, put on small flame and close lid for a while.

3. 撒柠檬汁，盛出，撒葱

1. 鲶鱼两面加盐和胡椒，中火慢煎出油脂。（由于鲶鱼较肥，锅里不用放油）

1. Add some salt & pepper to both sides of catfish. Use medium fire, slowly pan fry till the oil comes out. (No need to put oil in the pan)

2. 盛出鲶鱼，锅里放入切块的豆腐。稍煎一会后，再次放入鱼，加水炖煮。稍加盐。

2. Get the fish out, put in soft tofu and just pan fry for a little bit. Put fish back in, add water and bring to boil. Add some salt.

3. 在空碗里放葱花、香菜、胡椒、盐等，盛汤享用。

3. Get an empty bowl and add green onions, cilantro, pepper, salt, etc. Pour the soup in and enjoy!

做法一 Method 1

鸡块进行基本调味（料酒、蚝油、蒜粉、辣椒粉、盐、胡椒粉）之后下锅炒变色捞出。

Marinate chicken with basic spices (rice wine, oyster sauce, garlic powder, chili powder, salt and pepper). Pan fry till color change, then put it aside for later use.

炒土豆，放盐和辣椒粉，随意加点香料（dill, rosemary之类的），加水煮软

Pan fry potatos with salt, chili powder and optional dill/rosemary. Add water and boil till soft.

放入鸡块焖熟收汁

Add chicken and simmer until chicken is fully cooked. Then evaporate excess water.

(土豆的主要作用是提供淀粉，让鸡块更嫩)

(The main idea is to use the starch from the potatoes to make the chicken taste more tender)

做法二 Method 2

鸡块洗净，与土豆块、胡萝卜块一同放入锅中。选用带脂肪的鸡腿肉，增添汤汁中的胶质。

Put chopped chicken, potatos and carrots into the same pot. Use fatty meat from chicken legs to increase the stickiness of the soup.

加少量水、料酒、老抽、八角、盐，炖煮1小时左右

Add a little water, rice wine, dark soy sauce, star anise and salt. Cook with lid for around 1 hour.

收汁后完成。

Evaporate excessive water and serve.

鸡肉尽量切小块，用酱油、淀粉、酒、盐腌一下。（淀粉重要，作用等于嫩肉粉）

Cut chicken into small pieces, marinate with soy sauce, starch, rice wine and salt. Starch is important because it can make the meat tender.

切出大蒜碎、洋葱碎、番茄碎

Prepare chopped garlic, onions and tomatoes

起锅先炒大蒜洋葱，随后鸡肉。基本炒熟后加入番茄。再加入牛奶、希腊酸奶、咖喱粉、辣椒粉、盐。煮好之后撒上柠檬汁出锅。

Pan fry garlic and onions, then add chicken. When chicken is almost fully cooked, add tomatoes, and then milk, yogurt, curry powder, chili powder and salt. Once ready, sprinkle lemon juice as the last step.

鸡肉加料酒、酒酿、盐腌一腌

Marinate chicken with rice wine, fermented rice and salt

蒜片+盐和酱油炒白萝卜，随后另起一锅加水煮。

Pan fry radish with salt and soy sauce. Then transfer to another pot and boil with water.

炒鸡肉（放盐）和腊肠，然后加入锅中一起煮，放入鹌鹑蛋

Pan fry chicken (with salt) and Chinese sweet sausage. Then add to the radish soup and boil together. Add boiled and peeled quail eggs too.

收汁，调整盐度，加入香菜碎出锅。

Evaporate excess water. Adjust the salt level. Add cilantro and serve.

注：原型为腌笃鲜。鲜肉->鸡肉。咸肉->腊肠。春笋->白萝卜。

Use hand to press and mix power greens, garlic salt, lemon juice, oil, and seasoned soy sauce for seafood.

再加入切成小块的番茄、少量洋葱丝，以及些许芝士碎，一起摇晃均匀。

Add tomatos cut into small cubes, some shredded onion and shredded cheese. Shake and mix well.

Put cooked fish on top of salad and enjoy~

Stir fry finely chopped tomatoes until juicy. Add water, a little rice wine, a little bean paste, a little seasoned soy sauce for seafood. Bring to boil.

放入切好片的龙利鱼，焖煮一段时间。最后撒葱出锅。

Add flounder fillets, boil with lid for some time. Once done, add spring onions and serve.

选择带皮的厚三文鱼排。鱼皮面抹上蒜盐、胡椒、莳萝；鱼肉面抹上蒜盐和莳萝。

Choose salmon steak with skin. On the skin side, sprinkle garlic salt, pepper and dill. On the flesh side, sprinkle garlic salt and dill.

先用中高火将鱼皮面煎脆。然后转小火，盖锅盖，慢煎至熟。可以用锅铲切开中间来加速。

First pan-fry the skin till crispy with medium high flame. Then cover with lid and turn to small flame, slowly grill salmon till fully-cooked. To accelerate the process, cut the steak from the middle with spatula.

撒柠檬汁出锅。剩下的鱼油可以用来炒一点蔬菜~

Add lemon juice and serve. Use the remaining fish oil to cook some veggies!

选择薄切的牛排，两面抹上蒜盐、胡椒、莳萝。高火将两面煎熟，以保证中间的肉不老。

Choose thin-sliced steak. Add garlic salt, pepper and dill to both sides. Grill both sides with high flame and make sure the middle remains tender.

肉沫加盐，中火炒出油脂后加蒜瓣、豆瓣酱，翻炒均匀。

Pan fry ground pork (with salt) till oil comes out. Then add garlic, bean paste and mix together.

加水下豆腐。加盐、酱油、油泼辣子焖煮。

Add water and tofu. Add salt, soy sauce, chili oil. Simmer with lid.

开盖收汁。收得差不多了加点醋，勾芡，洒葱，出锅。

Open the lid and evaporate excess water. When almost ready, add a bit of vinegar, thicken the soup with starch, sprinkle green onions, and serve.

黄油、胡萝卜、土豆、洋葱或大葱，入锅翻炒

Pan fry butter, carrots, potatos, and onions (or leek)

加入牛奶焖煮（替代水和淡奶油），煮软后加三文鱼块

Add milk (as replacement of water + cream) and simmer. When ingredients become soft enough, add salmon.

最后加入盐、胡椒和莳萝上桌~

Add salt, pepper and dill. Serve!

1. 先用刨子刨一些柠檬皮到米饭里。然后将柠檬去蒂切成4块放进电饭煲。放芥菜籽、小豆蔻、足够的盐。开始煮饭。

1. Peel some lemon skin into the rice. Then cut the lemon into 4 pieces and put into the rice cooker. Also put mustard seeds, cardamom and enough salt. Start to cook the rice.

2. 饭煮好后倒入炒锅中炒一段时间，用高温去除柠檬和芥菜籽的苦味。同时撒上姜黄粉上色。可以尝一尝柠檬皮，直到没有苦味即可盛出。

2. Once rice is cooked, pour into a frying pan. Use the high temperature to remove the bitterness of lemon and mustard seeds. At the same time, add tumeric powder to color the rice. Taste the lemon skin - once it is no longer bitter, food is ready to serve.

1. 豆腐切片放入锅中，中小火煎至两面金黄。

1. Pan-fry tofu with medium flame until both sides turn golden.

2. 放入葱段，倒入酱汁（酱油、蚝油、黑胡椒、盐加水调开）煮一下，即可出锅。
