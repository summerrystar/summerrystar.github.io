做法一 Method 1

鸡块进行基本调味（料酒、蚝油、蒜粉、辣椒粉、盐、胡椒粉）之后下锅炒变色捞出。

Marinate chicken with basic spices (rice wine, oyster sauce, garlic powder, chili powder, salt and pepper). Pan fry till color change, then put it aside for later use.

炒土豆，放盐和辣椒粉，随意加点香料（dill, rosemary之类的），加水煮软

Pan fry potatos with salt, chili powder and optional dill/rosemary. Add water and boil till soft.

放入鸡块焖熟收汁

Add chicken and simmer until chicken is fully cooked. Then evaporate excess water.

(土豆的主要作用是提供淀粉，让鸡块更嫩)

(The main idea is to use the starch from the potatoes to make the chicken taste more tender)

做法二 Method 2

鸡块洗净，与土豆块、胡萝卜块一同放入锅中。选用带脂肪的鸡腿肉，增添汤汁中的胶质。

Put chopped chicken, potatos and carrots into the same pot. Use fatty meat from chicken legs to increase the stickiness of the soup.

加少量水、料酒、老抽、八角、盐，炖煮1小时左右

Add a little water, rice wine, dark soy sauce, star anise and salt. Cook with lid for around 1 hour.

收汁后完成。

Evaporate excessive water and serve.
