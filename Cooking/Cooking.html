1. 买肋排或带点肥的猪肉，煎至金黄（不用放油），放盐、酱油、酒，煎好盛出备用

1. Choose baby ribs or pork chops with some fat. Pan fry pork till golden (no need for oil). Put salt, soy sauce and rice wine. Remove from pan after ready.

2. 同一个锅下番茄炒软出汁（加盐），加开水，加猪肉煮开，焖10分钟

2. In the same pan, pan fry tomatos till it's soft and juicy (put salt). Add boiling water and pork. Let it boil with small fire for 10 minutes.

3. 尝盐度，补足盐后加白菜（不然白菜没味道），再焖5分钟直到白菜变软

3. Taste and add salt as needed, then add napa cabbage (add salt first or napa may not have enough taste). Boil with small fire for 5 more minutes till napa gets soft.

4. 可以加面条一起吃

4. Optionally add noodles

1. 蘑菇与米放进电饭锅一起煮，煮完加盐

1. Cook rice and mushroom together in the rice cooker. Add salt when finished

2. 煮饭的同时煎三文鱼：先把鱼皮煎到脆，然后可以捣碎了煎。调料用蒜盐、莳萝、一点柠檬汁。

2. While rice is getting cooked, pan fry salmon: First the skin side till crispy, then break into small pieces. Use garlic salt, dill and a sprinkle of lemon juice.

3. 组装三文鱼、蘑菇饭、足量葱花。三文鱼煎出的油恰好能使米饭颗粒分明。

3. Stir together salmon, mushroom rice, and enough chopped green onions. The oil produced while pan-frying the salmon can make rice grains separate from each other.

1. 鱼两面撒上蒜盐

1. Sprinkle garlic salt to both sides of the fish

2. 热锅内放食用油、黄油、蒜泥，下鱼，两面充分混合，煎至金黄。如果中间还没熟，再用小火焖一会儿

2. In a hot pan, put oil, butter, chopped garlic, put in fish, mix both sides properly, pan-fry till golden. If the inside is still raw, put on small flame and close lid for a while.

3. 撒柠檬汁，盛出，撒葱

3. Sprinkle lemon juice and green onions

1. 原料包括鲶鱼、嫩豆腐、葱花、盐、胡椒

1. Ingredients: catfish, silk tofu, spring onions, salt, pepper

2. 鲶鱼煎至金黄后，加水和盐，放入切成块的豆腐一起煮。由于鲶鱼较肥，煎的时候可以少放或不放油

2. Pan fry catfish to golden. Then add water and salt, boil with chopped silk tofu. Since catfish contains fat, put less or no oil while pan frying.

3. 待汤烧好后，在空碗里放葱花、胡椒、盐（若需要），盛汤享用。理想情况下汤呈淡淡的白色。

3. When soup is ready, get an empty bowl and add chopped green onions, pepper and salt (if needed). Then get the soup and enjoy! Ideally the soup should be cream-colored.

Marinate chicken with basic spices (rice wine, oyster sauce, garlic powder, chili powder, salt and pepper). Pan fry till color change, then put it aside for later use.

Pan fry potatos with salt, chili powder and optional dill/rosemary. Add water and boil till soft.

放入鸡块焖熟收汁

Add chicken and simmer until chicken is fully cooked. Then evaporate excess water.

Marinate chicken wings with soy sauce, chili powder, oyster sauce, rice wine, honey, garlic powder and pepper (and optionally salt).

放入空气炸锅360度烤20分钟

Air-fry with 360 degrees for 20 minutes

鸡肉尽量切小块，用酱油、淀粉、酒、盐腌一下。（淀粉重要，作用等于嫩肉粉）

Cut chicken into small pieces, marinate with soy sauce, starch, rice wine and salt. Starch is important because it can make the meat tender.

切出大蒜碎、洋葱碎、番茄碎

Prepare chopped garlic, onions and tomatoes

起锅先炒大蒜洋葱，随后鸡肉。基本炒熟后加入番茄。再加入牛奶、希腊酸奶、咖喱粉、辣椒粉、盐。煮好之后撒上柠檬汁出锅。

Pan fry garlic and onions, then add chicken. When chicken is almost fully cooked, add tomatoes, and then milk, yogurt, curry powder, chili powder and salt. Once ready, sprinkle lemon juice as the last step.

Pan fry radish with salt and soy sauce. Then transfer to another pot and boil with water.

Pan fry chicken (with salt) and Chinese sweet sausage. Then add to the radish soup and boil together. Add boiled and peeled quail eggs too.
